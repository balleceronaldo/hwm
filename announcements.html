Latest Announcements

Official updates from CEPMO regarding hazardous waste management, policy changes, collection schedules, and important notices.

Stay informed — check this page regularly for the latest news and updates!
December 15, 2025

Current Accepted Items Update

As of today, the facility is accepting ONLY: Used Oil, Busted Fluorescent Lamps (BFL), E-Waste, and Expired Medicines. Other hazardous waste items will be accepted in future scheduled collection events.

November 20, 2025

Low-Cost Monthly Drop-Off Service Launched

We are proud to announce a new ₱10 per month per household drop-off service for accepted hazardous waste items. Visit the facility during operating hours.

October 5, 2025

Upcoming Expansion Announcement

Construction of the full Hazardous Waste Storage Facility in Dontogan is expected to begin in 2026. This will allow acceptance of more types of household hazardous waste.

More announcements will be posted here as they become available.
