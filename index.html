Welcome

Current Status (December 2025): The facility accepts ONLY:
• Used Oil • Busted Fluorescent Lamps (BFL) • E-Waste • Expired Medicines
Other items (e.g., batteries, paints, cleaners, pesticides) are NOT accepted. Contact your barangay or CEPMO for alternatives.

The Baguio Hazardous Waste Storage Facility, managed by the City Environment and Parks Management Office (CEPMO) under Atty. Rhenan G. Diwas (Head and Managing Head) and Engr. Wilbur S. Suanding (Pollution Control Officer and WHWMD Head), includes the operational Bakakeng Central TSD and the planned The4E Facility for future expansion.

Only these four items are currently accepted at the facility.

Used Oil

Description: Used motor or cooking oil that can pollute soil and water.

Disposal: Bring in sealed containers to the facility or collection events.

Busted Fluorescent Lamps (BFL)

Description: Broken CFLs or tubes containing mercury.

Disposal: Store carefully in a box; bring to facility (handle intact ones similarly).

E-Waste

Description: Old electronics (phones, computers, appliances) with toxic components.

Disposal: Drop at Bakakeng Central or collection events.

Expired Medicines

Description: Unused or expired drugs that can contaminate water if flushed.

Disposal: Bring to facility take-back programs (do not flush or trash).

Facilities managed by CEPMO: Bakakeng Central (operational, focus on e-waste/BFL) and The4E (planned for expansion, including used oil and expired medicines).
Contact CEPMO for schedules and requirements.
