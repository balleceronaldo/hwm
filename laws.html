Hazardous Waste Laws & Regulations

Key national laws, republic acts, and administrative orders governing hazardous waste management in the Philippines. These form the legal foundation for Baguio City's local program.

National Laws & Regulations

  • Republic Act No. 6969 – Toxic Substances and Hazardous and Nuclear Wastes Control Act of 1990
    Primary law regulating the importation, manufacture, processing, handling, storage, transportation, sale, distribution, use, and disposal of toxic chemicals and hazardous wastes.
    Read full text on Official Gazette
  • Republic Act No. 9003 – Ecological Solid Waste Management Act of 2000
    Provides the legal framework for solid waste management, including special/hazardous wastes from households and institutions.
    Read full text on Official Gazette
  • Republic Act No. 8749 – Philippine Clean Air Act of 1999
    Regulates air pollution from hazardous substances, including emissions from improper waste burning.
    Read full text on Official Gazette
  • Republic Act No. 9275 – Philippine Clean Water Act of 2004
    Protects water bodies from pollution caused by improper disposal of hazardous wastes.
    Read full text on Official Gazette
  • DENR Administrative Order No. 2013-22 – Revised Procedures and Standards for the Management of Hazardous Wastes
    Detailed implementing rules for RA 6969, including classification, registration, transport, treatment, and disposal of hazardous wastes.
    Download PDF from DENR-EMB
  • DENR Administrative Order No. 2021-19 – Updated List of Hazardous Wastes
    Latest classification and waste numbers (e.g., M506 for e-waste, M503 for pharmaceuticals).
    Contact DENR-EMB for latest version.
These national laws are supplemented by Baguio City's local ordinance (see Ordinance page) for localized implementation.
